Basketball

More than a Game

About Basketball

Basketball is a dynamic sport played worldwide, known for its speed, skill, and strategy. From the NBA to local leagues, it inspires millions with thrilling matches and iconic players.

History

Basketball was formed in 1891 by by James Naismith at the International YMCA Training School in Springfield, Massachusetts, using peach baskets as goals.
Naismith published 13 original rules, emphasizing passing, non-contact play, and stationary ball handling.
The first public game occurred on March 11, 1892, in Springfield, Massachusetts.

Basketball spread rapidly through YMCA networks to Canada, France, England, Australia, China, India, and Japan by the early 1900s The 5-a-side rule was established in 1897
The first professional league formed in 1898, though early pro basketball grew slowly making its olympic debut in 1936 After World War II, the sport expanded internationally, especially in Europe and Asia with the NBA being formed in 1949 Today Basketball is one of the world's most popular sports, played globally and governed internationally by FIBA

Throughout the years many stars have been made from the sport such as:

  • Michael Jordan
  • Lebron james
  • Stephen Curry
  • Kawahi Leonard
  • Kobe Bryant
