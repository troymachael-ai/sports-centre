About athletics

Athletics is a collection of sporting events that involve running, jumping, throwing, and walking. It is one of the oldest forms of organized sport, dating back to ancient Greece, where it was a central part of the Olympic Games. Today, athletics includes events such as sprints, marathons, long jump, high jump, shot put, and javelin throw. These sports not only test physical strength and endurance but also require discipline, strategy, and mental focus. Athletics promotes fitness, teamwork, and healthy competition, making it an important part of both school programs and international sporting events.
